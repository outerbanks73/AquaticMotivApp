## Before You Start

- Use Shopify Admin.
- Edit only the five blog posts listed in this file.
- Do not change article titles, handles, images, excerpts, tags, or publish dates.
- Do not create redirects.
- Do not create new pages.
- Do not use code view or HTML mode.
- If the article starts looking broken, stop and ask for help before saving.

## How To Edit One Article

1. Log in to Shopify Admin.
2. Use the Shopify search bar to search for the article title.
3. Open the blog post.
4. Click inside the main article body.
5. Make a quick backup:
   - Click inside the article body.
   - Select all body content.
   - Copy it.
   - Paste it into a temporary Google Doc or local note named `BEFORE - [article title]`.
6. Scroll near the lower third of the article.
7. Add the `Landing page sentence` from the matching article section below.
8. Highlight only the listed `link words`.
9. Click the link button.
10. Paste the listed URL.
11. Add the `Shop sentence`.
12. Highlight only the listed `link words`.
13. Click the link button.
14. Paste the listed URL.
15. Near the end of the article, add a heading: `Related Care Guides`.
16. Under that heading, add the related guide titles as a bulleted list.
17. Highlight each related guide title and add its listed URL.
18. Use Preview if Shopify allows it.
19. If Shopify requires saving first, save and then open the live article immediately.
20. Check every new link and make sure the page looks normal.

## Final Check For Each Article

- The article has one new link to the main landing page.
- The article has one new shop link.
- The article has a `Related Care Guides` list.
- Every related guide title is clickable.
- No paragraph looks duplicated.
- No formatting looks broken on mobile preview.
